The Silent Imbalance

Every day, offerings are made with shraddha and bhakti in our temples and sacred rivers. Yet, without mindful care, even shuddh sankalp can disturb prakriti, and the many jeevs within. Dharma reminds us — all of creation is sacred.

These offerings, though shuddh in sankalp, when left without proper maryada and samvedana, can disturb natural harmony.

Dharma teaches us that all of creation is sacred. Our devotion must align with this truth — it should poshan (nurture), not ahimsa (harm).

Let our bhakti be guided by wisdom, so that it preserves both the divine and the world that sustains it.

Devotion should nurture, not harm.
